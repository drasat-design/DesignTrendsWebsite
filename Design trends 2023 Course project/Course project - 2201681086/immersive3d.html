Immersive 3d

Last year, many websites favored immersive scrolling experiences that pulled viewers into the page. Partially thanks to a continued interest in VR tech, this trend has now evolved into completely immersive websites—fully rendered 3D worlds.


While these website worlds tend to be more stylistic than realistic, that is exactly what makes them appealing—they are imaginative, escapist spaces that users will want to lose themselves in. At their best, they challenge the many stale layouts that orient the page around a CTA button. These websites trust the visual experience to leave a lasting mark on visitors instead.
